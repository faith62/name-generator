Name Generator

This is a website that takes in the user's birthday date, thereafter calculates the day of the week one is born. Depending on the gender: male or female it outputs one's Akan Name

Akan names are used by the Akan people of Ghana and Ivory Coast.Frequently in Ghana, children are being given their first names as 'day name' in which it corressponds to the day of the week one is born.
